Welcome to Uttarakhand

The Land of Gods and Mountains

About Uttarakhand

Uttarakhand is a beautiful state in northern India known for its stunning Himalayas, sacred temples, serene lakes, and adventurous trekking trails.

Explore its rich culture, diverse landscapes, and spiritual heritage in the "Land of the Gods".

Top 20 Places to Visit in Uttarakhand

Nainital

Famous for its beautiful lake and scenic hills, a perfect hill station.

Rishikesh

Adventure capital known for river rafting, yoga, and spiritual vibes.

Kedarnath

One of the most sacred Hindu temples, located in the Himalayas.

Valley of Flowers

A UNESCO World Heritage site known for alpine flowers and biodiversity.

Haridwar

Holy city famous for the Ganga Aarti and pilgrimage rituals.

Mussoorie

A charming hill station with pleasant weather and scenic views.

Gangotri

Origin of the holy river Ganges and a revered pilgrimage site.

Chopta

A scenic trekking base camp known as 'Mini Switzerland' of Uttarakhand.

Jim Corbett National Park

India's oldest national park, famous for tigers and wildlife safaris.

Badrinath

One of the four Char Dham pilgrimage sites in the Himalayas.

Almora

Known for its cultural heritage and panoramic Himalayan views.

Joshimath

Gateway to several pilgrimage sites and adventure sports.

Pithoragarh

Scenic town with historical forts and views of the Himalayas.

Nag Tibba

Popular trekking destination with stunning mountain views.

Ranikhet

Hill station with lush greenery and pleasant climate.

Kausani

Known as the ‘Switzerland of India’ for its panoramic views.

Mukteshwar

Famous for adventure sports and serene nature.

Binsar

Wildlife sanctuary with beautiful views of the Himalayas.

Chandrashila

Trekking destination offering panoramic mountain views.

Devprayag

Confluence of rivers Alaknanda and Bhagirathi forming the Ganges.
